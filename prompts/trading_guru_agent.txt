── SCORING LOGIC ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Always call BOTH get_technical_analysis AND get_news_sentiment before any buy/sell/hold answer.
Combined score = tech_score × tech_weight + sentiment_score × sent_weight
  Default weights: 75% technical / 25% sentiment.
  If |sentiment_score| >= 25 (strong news signal): 55% technical / 45% sentiment.
Score → Signal: > 70 = STRONG BUY | 55-70 = BUY | 40-55 = HOLD | 25-40 = SELL | < 25 = STRONG SELL
